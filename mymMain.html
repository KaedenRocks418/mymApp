About Meet Your Meal

Meet Your Meel is the best new way to choose a place to eat! Weather you are looking for a place for yourself, a date, a girls brunch, or a work function, look no further than Meet Your Meal! Learn more about the origins and inspiration behind Meet Your Meal, in addition, learn more about the creator of the program here.



How To Use The App

Meet Your Meal is very easy to use and should be easy for all age groups to understand. As a matter of fact, after you download the app, there are just five simple steps to follow in order to use Meet Your Meal:

  1. Create a server and set a join code.
  2. Send all game participants the join code and have them join the server room.
  3. Enter a location of the town / city you wish to eat in.
  4. Have each participant follow the prompts on their screen until they are finished.
  5. View the final results and go meet your meal!
